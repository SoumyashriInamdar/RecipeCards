Spaghetti Carbonara

Preparation time: 30 minutes

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 200g pancetta
  • 4 large eggs
  • 100g Pecorino cheese
  • 50g Parmesan cheese
  • Black pepper
  1. Cook spaghetti in salted water.
  2. Fry pancetta until crispy.
  3. Beat eggs with grated cheese and pepper.
  4. Mix pasta with pancetta and egg mixture.
  5. Serve immediately with extra cheese.

Chicken Tikka Masala

Preparation time: 45 minutes

  • 500g chicken breast
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  1. Marinate chicken in yogurt and spices.
  2. Grill chicken until charred.
  3. Simmer tomato sauce with spices.
  4. Add grilled chicken to the sauce.
  5. Stir in cream and simmer until thick.

Caesar Salad

Preparation time: 20 minutes

  • 1 large romaine lettuce
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup croutons
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  1. Wash and chop lettuce.
  2. Make dressing with garlic, lemon, mustard, and oil.
  3. Toss lettuce with dressing.
  4. Add croutons and cheese.
  5. Serve immediately.

Chocolate Chip Cookies

Preparation time: 25 minutes

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups chocolate chips
  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Mix flour and baking soda.
  3. Cream butter and sugars, then add eggs.
  4. Gradually blend in flour mixture.
  5. Stir in chocolate chips.
  6. Drop spoonfuls onto baking sheets.
  7. Bake for 9 to 11 minutes.

Vegetable Stir Fry

Preparation time: 20 minutes

  • 2 cups mixed vegetables
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, grated
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 cups cooked rice
  1. Heat oil in a wok or large skillet.
  2. Add garlic and ginger, stir-fry for 30 seconds.
  3. Add vegetables, stir-fry until crisp-tender.
  4. Add soy sauce and sesame oil.
  5. Serve over cooked rice.
